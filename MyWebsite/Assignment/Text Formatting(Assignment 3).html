You Must Know This.

Light emitted from the sun is called white light, A.K.A Electromagnetic Spectrum.

Electromagnetic Spectrum contains the following waves: Gamma rays, X-rays, Ultraviolet(UV) waves, Visible light, Infrared ray, Microwaves, and Radio waves.

Light itself travels at 3 x 108m/s (300,000,000 m/s). Visible lights are the color we see: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and violet. Hence why they are called 'Visible' light.
